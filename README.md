**Local Service Marketplace**
Project Description:

KaamOnClick is a web-based platform that connects customers with local service providers. Customers can easily post their service requirements, and nearby workers can view the requests and place bids for the job. This platform simplifies the process of finding trusted local professionals for services such as plumbing, electrical work, cleaning, carpentry, and more.

The goal of this project is to make it easier for people to find reliable service providers in their area while giving local workers a platform to get more job opportunities.

**Problem Statement**

Many people struggle to find reliable local service providers quickly. Usually they depend on word-of-mouth recommendations or spend a lot of time searching online. At the same time, many skilled local workers find it difficult to reach potential customers and get consistent work opportunities.

There is a need for a platform that connects customers and service providers efficiently in one place.


**How Our Project Solves This Problem**

Our KaamOnClick solves this problem by providing a centralized platform where:

Customers can post service requests.
Service providers can view available jobs.
Workers can place bids for jobs.
Customers can compare bids and choose the best service provider.
Both customers and workers can manage their profiles and job activities through dashboards.
This system saves time for customers and helps local workers get more job opportunities.
